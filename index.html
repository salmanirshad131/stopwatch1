About This App

Professional Stopwatch is a modern, feature-rich timing application designed for precision and simplicity. Track your time with accuracy down to the second, record unlimited laps, and never lose your progress with auto-save functionality. Perfect for workouts, studying, cooking, or any activity requiring precise time tracking.

Features

• Accurate time tracking (HH:MM:SS format)
• Unlimited lap recording
• Auto-save functionality
• Dark and Light themes
• Modern, professional design
• Optimized for mobile devices
